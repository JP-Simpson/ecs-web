The Continental Shelf Recommendations Project provides an accessible record of recommendations made by the Commission on the Limits of the Continental Shelf. It collates information on the Commissions approaches and precedents with respect to the characteristics of continental margins and the application of Article 76 of the United Nations Convention on the Law of the Sea.
